About KVK Kupwara

Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) Kupwara is an agricultural extension center under the Indian Council of Agricultural Research, affiliated with a local agricultural university. We connect research with farmers by implementing advanced agricultural practices tailored to the local needs of Kupwara, promoting sustainable farming and productivity.
